The Iris Glass Pro: A Week With Apple's True Smart Glasses

Published on June 11, 2026 • Written by Tech Enthusiast

After years of concept renders and heavy developer headsets, the promise of true augmented reality has finally arrived on our faces in a frame that looks entirely normal.

The Iris Glass Pro weights just 48 grams. When you put them on, the real world doesn't disappear. Instead, crisp digital text and directions float seamlessly over your actual environment. Reading notifications while walking down the street feels fluid, fast, and surprisingly non-intrusive.

Battery life is the real shocker here. Thanks to a highly efficient custom chipset, these glasses easily lasted through an entire 14-hour workday of navigation, notifications, and quick audio calls before needing to snap back into their charging case.

9.2 / 10

The Verdict: The Future is Finally Here

While the RM3,500 price tag will keep it out of reach for casual consumers, early adopters and productivity tech fans will find it an incredible glimpse into a screenless future.
